## Task: Apply basic visual styling

**Goal**

Make the Home and Story screens feel cohesive and readable using the existing theme utilities, without full visual polish yet.

**Description**

- Use `ThemedView`, `ThemedText`, and any existing design tokens from `constants/theme.ts` to style:
  - Screen backgrounds.
  - Titles and section headings.
  - Campaign cards on the Home screen.
  - Story text and choice buttons on the Story screen.
- Ensure spacing, padding, and font sizes are comfortable on a typical mobile device.
- Keep the color usage consistent between screens.

**Acceptance Criteria**

- Home and Story screens share a consistent look-and-feel (colors, typography, and spacing).
- Campaign rows and choices are clearly tappable and visually distinct.
- No obvious layout issues on common device sizes in Expo Go.

**Notes**

- Detailed visual refinement, animations, and custom components can be scheduled for later sprints.
